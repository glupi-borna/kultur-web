KulTur

KulTur is a non-profit NGO founded in 2021 in Velika Gorica. The aim of the organization is the promotion, exploration and research of culture and art.

We are engaged in cultural enrichment, art workshops, education and seminars. We organize events and festivals to promote young artists and make culture more accessible to the wider community.

We pay special attention to the education and mental health of the most vulnerable groups.

If you want to become a member of our organization, contact us by e-mail.
